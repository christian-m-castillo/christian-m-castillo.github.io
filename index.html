About

I'm passionate about crafting exceptional online experiences. I'm well-versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Flutter, and a range of frameworks and tools. Personal History

Before becoming a developer, I served for 8 years as a U.S. Air Force Aeromedical Evacuation Technician (fancy speak for flight medic) and I ran a portrait photography business.

My Air Force flight medic journey was a chapter marked by dedication, leadership-building, and test-of-grit commitment to service. This experience instilled discipline, resilience, and a cool-headed approach to high pressure operations. It gave me a profound appreciation for clear communication and teamwork: skills that are highly invaluable in both healthcare and commerce.

For me, the art of photography extends beyond mere pastime or occupation; it's a channel to crystalize the fleeting, conjure poems of the soul, and currate life stories. From sweeping landscapes to intimate portraits, image-making has given me a unique eye for detail and aesthetics. My experience as a photographer has taught me the importance of visual storytelling and design, skills that seamlessly integrate into my development work.
