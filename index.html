Personal History

Before becoming a developer, I served for 8 years as a U.S. Air Force Aeromedical Evacuation Technician (fancy speak for flight medic) and I ran a portrait photography business.

My Air Force flight medic journey instilled discipline, resilience, and a cool-headed approach to high pressure operations. It gave me a profound appreciation for clear communication and teamwork: skills that are highly invaluable in both healthcare and commerce.

Photography is a channel to crystalize the fleeting, conjure poems of the soul, and currate life stories. My experience as a photographer taught me the importance of visual storytelling, skills that seamlessly integrate into my development and design work.
